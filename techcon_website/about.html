Our History

TechCon began in early 2025 as a grassroots gathering of developers and innovators. Over the past few months, it has grown into a global platform for tech leaders, startups, and educators to share ideas and shape the future.

Our Mission

TechCon exists to empower the next generation of tech creators. We believe in open knowledge, inclusive innovation, and collaborative growth across borders and disciplines.

Notable Past Speakers

Dr. Amina Yusuf – Renowned AI researcher and advocate for ethical machine learning. Her keynote on responsible AI in 2022 sparked global conversations.

Carlos Mendez – Blockchain pioneer and founder of ChainBridge. He shared insights on decentralized finance and tech equity in 2021.

Lina Chen – Cybersecurity strategist and author. Her 2020 session on digital resilience remains one of the most viewed TechCon talks.
